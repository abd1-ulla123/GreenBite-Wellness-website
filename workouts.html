Why Regular Exercise Matters

Regular exercise improves your overall health, boosts energy, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases. It strengthens muscles and bones, enhances mood, and supports better sleep. Even short daily workouts can create lasting positive changes in your life.

Did You Know?

  • Just 30 minutes of daily exercise can reduce the risk of heart disease by 40%.
  • Strength training boosts metabolism, helping you burn calories even at rest.
  • Exercise releases endorphins — your body’s natural “happy hormones”.

    Tips for Effective Workouts

    • Always warm up before exercising.
    • Stay hydrated before, during, and after workouts.
    • Focus on form over speed — quality matters most.
    • Rest days are just as important as workout days.
    • Set realistic goals and track your progress.

    Different Types of Workouts

    Cardio

    Improves heart health and burns calories (running, cycling, HIIT).

    Strength

    Builds muscle and increases power (weights, resistance training).

    Flexibility

    Improves mobility and reduces injury risk (yoga, stretching).
